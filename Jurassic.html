    Saurischians

    • Name means 'reptile hipped' and is the other branch of the two basic divisions of dinosaurs:
      • the other being ornithischia
    • This group includes all theropods and the sauropods:
      • Theropods are almost entirely carnivorous dinos, few weirdd omnivores and herbivores evolving in the Cretaceous
      • Sauropods are the long-necked dinosaurs, and are the largest known land animals to have ever been known to exist
        • All are herbivorous

    Sauropods

    • Name means 'lizard-footed' and are characterized by long necks and tails, large sizze, and were quadrupedal:
      • Were the largest land animals to have ever existed
    • Were able to grow to such large sizes in part due to the structure of their bones:
      • Theropod counterparts, had hollow bones
      • Had pneumatics sacs which surrounded their bones, making them lighter for their size
    • Were common in the Jurassic, but decline dramatically in the cretaceous

    Theropoda
