1. Design for Moore's Law: States that integrated circuit resources double every 18-24 months. As computer designs can take years the resources available per chip can easily double or quadruple over the duration of a project.
  1. Use Abstraction to simplify design: Without abstraction design time would lengthen as dramatically as resource growth shown by moore's law.
  1. Make the Common Case Fast:
  1. Performance via Parallelism
  2. Performance via Pipelining
  3. Performance via Prediction
  4. Hierarchy of Memories
  5. Dependability via redundancy
