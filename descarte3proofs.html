The setting of proofs is key: Introduces two ideas:

  1. Objective/formal reality of ideas
  2. The principle of causality

Formal/objective reality of ideas formal reality: pertians to the form of ideas: as ideas all ideas are the same

objective: pertains to the content of ideas: objective reality of an ideas depends on the reality of the object it represents.

Three kinds of object: Accident, Substance, Infinite Substance

The principle of causality: a cause has at least as much reality as its effect. as applied to the obj reality of ideas: the objective reality of an idea is caused by the reality it represents the degree of reality of the rep object is at least as great as the degree of reality of its idea.

Argumentative strategy: could i be the cause of this idea?

Proof by elimination

Could i be the cause of the following ideas? 1-thinking thing 2- living beings 3-corporeal inanimate things 4- god i have the idea of god, only god could cause this idea therefore god exists
