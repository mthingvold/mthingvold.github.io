The Sun

Star is the centre of the solar system Mostly hydrogen - rest helium

  • generates heat by fusing hydrogen into helium
  • When out of hydrogen fuses helium into carbon
    • becomes red giant
  • once helium is used will begin to fuse carbon into iron, shrinks to become a white dwarf
  • Once all carbon has fused into iron, a large star will become a supernova
    • the core of the sun would be crushed into a neutron star.
    • For very large stars ( much larger than our sun) this would create a black hole.
